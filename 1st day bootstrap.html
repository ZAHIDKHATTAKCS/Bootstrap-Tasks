Imran Khan

Imran Ahmed Khan Niazi HI PP (Punjabi and Urdu: عمران احمد خان نیازی, Pashto: عمران احمد خان نیازی; born 5 October 1952) is a Pakistani politician and former cricketer who is the 22nd[n 1] and current prime minister of Pakistan. He is also the chairman of the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI).
